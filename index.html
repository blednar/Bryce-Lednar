Highlighted Experiences

I've learned important lessons from each of my professional experiences. Below is a selection of the ones that have been particularly meaningful for my professional development.

QUID | CLIENT SERVICES DIRECTOR

Quid is an artificial intelligence technology company focused on text analytics that has raised $100M in funding from investors including Founders Fund and Atomico. Quid serves Fortune 500, Agency, and Consulting customers including Walmart, Brunswick, and BCG.

At Quid, I worked with a team of data scientists and analysts to help Walmart gain new data-driven perspectives on American families by identifying trends across forum discussions, media coverage, venture funding, and Walmart transaction data.
